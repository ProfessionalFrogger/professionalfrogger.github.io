The Lore

The Name

    Yes, "ProfessionalFrogger" is a bunny name. Yes, it's been that way for years. No, I'm not changing it. The origin story is lost to antiquity. One day I'll remember it.

The History

    I've been a furry since I was five or six. My first fursona was an unnamed cheetah when I was in kindergarden. We were dressing like animals for a play and I picked a cheetah. Come to think of it, the rest of my class was technically furry for a day. 'evilgrin.png'
    When I was nine, my interest in the furry fandom itself started with the release of Star Fox 64. The idea that animals could walk, talk and act as humans was facinating to me.

The Design

    Soft tawny-brown coat with lighter underfur. Inner ears are a lighter. I have an inexplicable obsession with purple.

Personality

    Playful and chaotic with bursts of unexpectedly profound takes. Loves long walks along the shores of Cape Claw, snacks, and finding the real reason why earphone cables tangle in your pocket.

Fun Facts

    I can smell a sequel from miles away. I watch movies till the after credits. I'm usually one of the last ones out of the theatre. Thinks the tutorial should always be skippable.(Except in Driver - get gud)
